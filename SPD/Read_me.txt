In the SPD documents you have the SPD files obtained from a random subject after filtering with various filtre. In X you have the windows, Y the frequencies
(specified in frequencies.mat) and Z the different electrodes. The window frequency should be 16Hz and initial sampling 512 Hz
Event_window contains informations for each trials: cue,fixation start, fixation end, feedback start, feeback end
Window_label: contains the event number for each time point
